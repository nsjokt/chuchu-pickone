This policy covers the build with ads (Google AdMob) and in-app purchases.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect directly identifying personal information (name, email, phone, precise location). To serve ads (which keep the app free), Google AdMob may automatically process an advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID / iOS IDFA), device type, OS, approximate region, and ad-interaction data, used solely for ad delivery, frequency capping, fraud prevention, and performance measurement. Buying “Remove Ads” stops ads and the related identifier processing.

2. Game Data Storage

Game progress (coins, owned characters, purchases, high scores) is stored only on your device. It is never sent to our servers and is deleted when you uninstall.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (ads) — privacy
  • Google Play Billing — handled by the store; we never receive payment-instrument details.

4. In-App Purchases

Exactly ONE purchase: “Remove Ads” (non-consumable). The single currency “coins” is earned only by play, never sold, no cash-out value (no gems, no gacha).

5. Children’s Privacy

Rated for all ages; not directed at children for marketing. Ads are requested in non-personalized / age-appropriate mode.

6–8. Your Choices · Changes · Contact

You can reset/delete your advertising ID in device settings, or buy “Remove Ads” to turn ads off. We update this page on changes. Contact: systrade0625@gmail.com
